Child Safety Policy

Our commitments to protecting children and preventing exploitation or abuse on the Omegram platform.

Effective: January 1, 2024 Last Updated: March 2026
Our Commitment

Zero Tolerance for Child Exploitation

Omegram has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward any content, behavior, or activity that sexualizes, exploits, endangers, or involves minors in any way. This policy is absolute, non-negotiable, and applies to all users, all content types, and all features of the platform.

🔞

Adults Only Platform

Omegram is designed exclusively for users aged 18 and older. No features or content are directed at children.

🚫

CSAM Prohibition

Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) is absolutely prohibited. Any detected instance is immediately reported to authorities.

🛡️

Active Moderation

Our admin panel enables active content moderation and rapid removal of reported content involving minors.

📞

Report & Respond

We provide clear reporting mechanisms and respond to child safety reports within 24 hours.

Age Requirements

Age Restriction & Verification

Omegram is an 18+ platform. All users must confirm they are 18 years of age or older during the registration process.

⚠️ Important: Users who are found to be under 18 will have their accounts permanently suspended and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

Age enforcement measures include:

  • Age confirmation during account registration
  • Reporting mechanisms allowing other users to flag suspected underage accounts
  • Admin-level account review and suspension capability
  • Permanent ban on accounts confirmed to belong to minors

We acknowledge that no age verification system is completely infallible. We strongly encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's device activity and report any suspected use of Omegram by a minor immediately.

What Is Prohibited

Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on Omegram and will result in immediate account termination and, where applicable, reporting to law enforcement:

  • Any content depicting, describing, or simulating the sexual exploitation of minors (CSAM)
  • Grooming behavior — using the platform to build inappropriate relationships with minors
  • Soliciting personal information from users who appear to be under 18
  • Sharing or distributing content that sexualizes individuals under 18
  • Using Omegram to recruit or direct minors to any external platform or communication channel
  • Any form of coercion, manipulation, or trafficking targeting minors
  • Impersonating a minor or misrepresenting one's age to circumvent our policies
Legal Notice: Content that sexually exploits minors is illegal in virtually every jurisdiction. We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies worldwide. IP addresses, account data, and content logs will be provided to authorities upon lawful request.
How We Enforce

Detection & Enforcement

We use the following measures to detect and address violations of this Child Safety Policy:

  • User reporting: Any post, story, profile, or chat can be reported directly within the app
  • Admin review: Our admin panel provides tools to review reported content and take action
  • Verified-user posting: Only verified (blue-tick) users can publish posts and stories, reducing anonymous abuse
  • Account suspension: Accounts violating this policy are permanently suspended without appeal
  • Data preservation: Relevant account data is preserved for law enforcement upon request
✅ CSAM Reporting: Any confirmed Child Sexual Abuse Material discovered on our platform will be immediately reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) via their CyberTipline, in compliance with 18 U.S.C. § 2258A, as well as to relevant Turkish authorities.
For Parents & Guardians

Parental Guidance

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created an account on Omegram or has been exposed to harmful content on our platform, please act immediately:

  • Contact us at omegramdev@gmail.com with the subject line "MINOR ACCOUNT REPORT"
  • Provide the username or registered email address if known
  • We will suspend the account within 24 hours of a confirmed minor report
  • All personal data associated with the minor will be permanently deleted within 72 hours of confirmation

We also recommend contacting your local law enforcement or child protection agency if you believe your child has been the victim of exploitation or abuse facilitated through any online platform.
